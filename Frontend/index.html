About Us

Welcome to FaceTrack, the innovative attendance system that utilizes facial recognition technology to streamline the process of tracking student attendance. Our web and mobile application allows teachers and administrators to quickly and easily take attendance by hovering the camera in front of students' faces. The camera captures images of the students' faces and our facial recognition algorithm matches the captured images with the registered students' facial data. The result is an accurate and efficient attendance record that is automatically generated.

  • Our facial recognition technology ensures accurate and automated attendance tracking
  • Our user-friendly web and mobile application makes attendance tracking easy and efficient.
  • We strive to make attendance tracking a seamless and effortless task for educators

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is FaceTrack?

    FaceTrack is a web and mobile application that uses facial recognition technology for attendance tracking. It captures the images of the students using the camera and generates an excel sheet of attendance.

  • How does FaceTrack work?

    FaceTrack uses facial recognition technology to identify and match faces with the registered list of students in the class. It marks the students as present or absent in the excel sheet.

  • Is FaceTrack only available as a web application?

    No, FaceTrack is also available as a mobile application.

  • Is FaceTrack secure?

    Yes, FaceTrack takes security measures to ensure data privacy for all its users.

  • Is FaceTrack a touchless system?

    Yes, FaceTrack uses touchless biometrics, such as face recognition, to keep people moving faster and reduce the number of surfaces touched to get access or track time.
